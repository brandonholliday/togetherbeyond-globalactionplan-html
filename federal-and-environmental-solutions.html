Global Action Plan Dashboard

In Q4 2022, Jacobs officially launched this TB Global Action Plan Dashboard to provide both transparency and track our progress towards achieving Jacobs Global Action Plan goals! On a quarterly basis, this dashboard will be updated by each region so we can track and learn more about our activities on a global, regional, and local level.

Want to get involved more or report a local activity? Contact your regional GAP Contact lead provided in each Region’s dashboard! Other ways to get involved include:

  1. Join a JEN through our e3 portal to connect with different communities and become a better Ally.
  2. Check the Action Plan box for related volunteer hours in Collectively, to help us track our progress.
  3. Join future events listed on inclusion calendar on the TB JacobsConnect page.
